MAGIC BUTTON CHEAT SHEET
========================
. = short press (1)    - = long press (0)
First press = Most Significant Bit (MSB)

Format: [button pattern] → (number) command name [params]

TIPS
----
- Wait 600ms (0.6 seconds) after completing a sequence
- For commands with parameters, enter the command first, wait for the sequence 
  to complete, then enter the parameter value as another binary sequence
- Short sequences are easier: use 1-9 for quick access to common commands
- Command 50 (zoomToColour) is special - it automatically cycles palettes
